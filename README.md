❗ Problem Statement
Farmers face several challenges:
• Lack of real-time crop guidance
• Difficulty identifying crop diseases
• Limited access to fair market prices
• Lack of farmer-to-farmer knowledge sharing
These issues reduce productivity and profitability.
💡 Our Solution
KRISHIVANTA provides an integrated platform that helps farmers with:
🌱 Smart Crop Guidance
📈 Market Price Information
👨‍🌾 Farmer Community Discussions
📚 Agricultural Knowledge Resources
This creates a complete ecosystem for farmers.
✨ Key Features
🌿 Crop Intelligence
Provides crop advice and disease identification support.
💰 Market Connect
Shows market price trends and selling opportunities.
👨‍🌾 Farmer Community
A discussion platform where farmers share experiences and solutions.
📚 Resource Hub
Guides, tips, and best agricultural practices.
